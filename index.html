The 3 Scrum artifacts

  • Product Backlog: The Product Backlog is an ordered list of user requirements.
  • Sprint Backlog: Sprint Backlog is created by the team members during the Sprint Planning meeting.
  • Sprint Burndown chart: Sprint Burndown chart is used to track the progress of a Sprint against a Sprint Backlog.
